classic Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is a comforting Italian d ish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and plenty of melted cheese. Perfect for family gatherings or a cozy dinner!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, sauté onion and garlic until translucent, then add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper, and simmer for 20 minutes to develop flavors.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles, then béchamel sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with béchamel and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
